### When to use: Prior to mission 4-A

REFERENCE: __Krellan Command and Battle Manual__
Planetary--Section 10.3, pages 73-76

## Starting The Invasion

1. Press [ESC] to return to the Strategic Command Menu
2. Press [P] for Planetary.
3. Ignore Bombardment 
	+ Select Orbit Control ([O])
![[7.1-invasion-to-orbit-control.gif]]
4. A planetary map will appear.
![[Pasted image 20210420194951.png]]
	+ Cities are displayed as X
	+ '+' are destroyed cities
5. Move your TDB box over an active city
6. Press [V]
![[7.1-invasion-view-city.gif]]
8. A map of the city appears, which can include:
	+ Various enemy units
	+ Phasers
	+ Shields
	+ Etc.
9. Press [ESC] to return to Invasion Control.
10. Press [B] for Bombard.
![[7.1-invasion-bombard-city.gif]]
12. The city is now destroyed
13. Repeat.
	+ Continue destroying cities until only four remain.
14. Move the TDB in turn to the remaining four cities.
15. press [S] each time to select them as invasion zones.
16. The city symbol will change to "[]".
	+ You do not need to view each city.
17. Press [I] to start the invasion.
	+ Make sure the four cities have been selected.

## The Invasion
1. You are in Invasion Control. 
2. The screen shows four invasion zones you have selected.
	+ Including four blank zones 
		+ a maximum of eight invasion zones are allowed.
3. Note the table shows that you have 16 shock troops and 28 warrior units in orbit.
	+ You also have 4 drop ships available.
4. NOTE: You can free and unfreeze (pause) the game at any time with [Alt][F]
5. The control keys [F1] through [F8] will display views of the invasion zones 1 - 8, respectively.
	+ In this mission, you will only use 1 - 4
6. [F9] and [F10]
	+ [F10] toggles between the current invasion zone and the Invasion Control screen.
	+ [F9] returns you to the Invasion Control screen.
7. The selected zone is indicated at the top right of the information panel, and is currently Zone 1.
	+ This zone can be changed by using the left and right arrow keys.
8. Only shock troops can start the invasion. 
9. Notes on shock troops and other units:
	+ Only shock troops can teleport to and from the planet's surface without using dropships.
	+ Shock troops main task is to construct a landing pad so that drop ships can transport warrior cohorts to the surface.
	+ Shock troops are elite but lightly armed cohorts.
	+ Warrior cohorts are heavily armed and more powerful than shock troops.
	+ Shock troops are more valuable than warrior cohorts because only shock troops can teleport.
	+ Conserve your Shock Troops, their ability to teleport makes them valuable.
## Begin Invasion - Assault Zone 1

1. Press [A] to Assault Zone 1.
2. Choose the northeast corner of the zone by pressing [2] (when asked).
3. Select 3 shock troop units. 
4. Confirm the assault.
	+ The invasion will begin.
	+ NOTE: 3 units is usually not enough to capture a zone, however, this zone is lightly defended.
5. Press [F1] to view Zone 1.
	+ Notice the ETA box towards the top right corner.
		+ This box displays the time of arrival of shock troops.
6. Return to Invasion Control ([F10] or [Esc]) and select invasion Zone 2 ([->]).
	+ Assault this zone with three shock troop units in the northwest corner.
7. Also send three units to Zones 3 and 4
	+ Select which parts of the zone to assault.
8. NOTE: The "In Transit" information changes as your shock troops leave orbit and beam down.
9. Go to Zone 1
	+ If your shock troops have not yet arrived, wait until they do.
	+ Using the cursor, select the unit which is farthest away from the enemy, then Order it ([0]) to Construct a landing pad ([C]).
	+ You are then told, in the ETA box, when the pad will be complete.
## You determine how units behave
1. You are allowed to determine, in general or specific terms, how units in each zone will behave.
2. You can list your options by:
 	+ By pressing [G] for General 
	+ and then [C] for Command.
3. If you want to order every individual unit yourself, press [D] for Direct.
4. Direct is usually unmanageable during any sizable invasion.
5. NOTE: The default setting is [P] for Protect.
	+ NOTE: Detailed descriptions are found on page 91 of the Battle Manual.
6. Select [R] for Reduce.

## Construction

1. Visit each zone one by one
2. Order the construction of a landing pad in each zone.
3. Try ordering different commands to each zone.

## Drop Ships

1. You will eventually receive a report from Zone 1 that the landing pad is complete.
	+ This is also indicated by a landing pad symbol to the left of the small zones map label.
2. Activate warriors 
	+ Select [D] / [B]
	+ then [W] / [6] to load six warrior cohorts on to the drop ship.
3. Select Zone 1 via left and right arrow keys
4. Press [L] to launch the ship to the selected zone.
5. Repeat for other zones as those landing pads are completed.

## If your landing is destroyed
1. Instruct one of the other shock troop cohorts in that zone to construct another one.
2. And if you really need to do so, send a few more shock troop cohorts down to fight the enemy while the construction is going on.

## When the drop ships land
1. You will be notified via report of the drop ship landing.
2. As the warriors disembark in a particular zone:
	+ Go to that zone 
	+ give the Standard command ([G] / [C] / [S]).
3. You can do this from the Invasion Control screen by selecting a particular zone and ordering troops in that zone via 
the Battle command ([B]).
4. You can also order a drop ship back to orbit by going to the zone map, selecting the drop ship, and ordering it to launch.

# Success

1. You will receive messages from each zone that the enemy in that zone has surrendered. 
2. You will then receive a message that the planet has been subjugated.
3. Command troops in each zone to Withdraw
	+ ([W]) using the Command option.
4. The shock troops will beam up and the warriors will board the drop ship in their zone.
5. If there is no drop ship in a zone, but warriors are still there:
	+ Send down an empty drop ship.
6. Return to the Invasion Control screen.
7. Await the arrival of all troops.
8. When all troops have arrived, press [ESC].
9. You will now receive a "Legionary Honors Report."
10. The higher honors go to victors in more difficult missions.
11. Leave the screen ([ESC]) and return to Orbit Control
12. At Orbit Control, you are given the option to Drop Off your legions.
	+ You have four legions in orbit.
	+ Drop two, just to keep the peace.
		+ Press [D]
	+ After two legions have been dropped off, press [H] to halt the transfer.
13. It is important to drop off some legions to hold the planet, in case the UGA shows up later to make a recapture attempt.
14. Return to orbit [ESC]
15. The planet's name is now Krellan green.
16. You receive a message of congratulations 
17. You receive a bonus of some slaves.
18. Quit the tutorial.
