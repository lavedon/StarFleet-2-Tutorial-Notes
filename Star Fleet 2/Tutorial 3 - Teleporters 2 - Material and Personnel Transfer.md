# Personnel Transfer
### Prior to mission 1-C
1. You start out in orbit around planet SS-16A
	1. KS-9 will re-supply you.
2. Your ship can hold up to 550 crew members, 300 shock troops, and 500 enemy captives.

## How to transfer personnel
1. Go to the Teleporters option ([E]/[T]
	1. Press [P] to transfer personnel.
	2. Move the Targeter over the supply ship
	3. Green numbers show people on our ship
	4. White shows the people you want to transfer.
	5. Press + to assign 20 shock troops to transfer
	6. Then select 20 crewmen
	7. Press [ENTER] to activate the teleporter
	## Material Transfer
	1. Very similar procedure to personnel transfer
	2. Move the TDB over a supply ship and press [ENTER]
	3. This brings up the material transfer display
	4. The highlight box is now the energy row
	5. Move the box over to the supply ship column and press [+] 20 times to assign 1000 units of energy.
	6. Next, assign 20 torpedoes, 185 units of supplies, 5 sensor probes, and 2 obliterator pods.
	7. When all maerials have been assigned, press [A] or [ENTER] to activate the teleporters.
