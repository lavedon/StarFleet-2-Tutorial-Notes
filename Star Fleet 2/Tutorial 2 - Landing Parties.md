# Tutorial 2 - Landing Parties
### When To Use: Priot to mission 1-B
### Purpose: Teach you how to assign your ship's personnel to various landing party missions, and use your teleporters to transfer them to a planet.
#### Reference: _Krellan Command and Battle Manual_ Teleporters - Section 5.2, pages 39-42

## Starting Out

1. You start in system SS-20
2. Go to the system map.
3. Open PLANETS data
4. Notice that two of the planets: B and C, are Class K (Krella-like), while planet A is Class F (Frozen).
		* Note: Landing parties can be put on any class of planet except Class G (Gaseous). 
5. Move to planet B
6. Establish orbit
	* Decline looking at a planetary map

## Assigning Teams

1. Survey the planet and set up a base.
2. Go to main display
3. Press [E] to enter Engineering Control
4. Press [T] for Teleporters
5. press [L] for Landing Parties
6. You should now see the Landing Parties Display
	i. The Landing Parties Display is used to assign teams to any of the six missions listed
			A. Teams are made up of 10 members
1. Press up down to highlight to select the Survey/Explore Mission
2. Press [+] three times to assign 3 team members to this mission.
3. Now assign three more teams to Negotiate
4. You can assign as many teams to your landing party missions as you want, as long as you have enough personnel aboard your ship.
### Assign Supplies to your teams
1.	At the bottom of the mission list - there is a supply category.
2.	Supply mission is used to assign supplies to be sent down with your landing parties.
3.	The planetary scan showed there were supplies available on this planet - so you do not have to send supplies down with your teams.

## Teleporting
1. Press [A] or [ENTER] to activate the teleporters.
2. As you continue to orbit the planet, you will recieve messages from your landing party.
		Example:
			1. They have started their mission
			2. They are under attack
			3. The planet's name is YUWXE
3. Note: You can tell the planet is neutral because the planet name appears in blue letters
## Deploy on another planet
1. Return to the System Map
2. Navigate to planet A
3. Establish oribt.
4. The scan shows that this planet is Class F.
	1. This planet has no intelligent lifeforms
	2. This planet has no supplies
5. Teleport three teams:
	1. Three teams to survey the planet
	2. Two teams to establish a base
	3. Since the scan shows no supplies available
		1. Move the highlight down to Suppy and assign 20 units using [+]
	4. Activate the teleporters
## Messages
1. Note: planet SS-20B is send you messages.
	1. They have established a base 
	2. Natives have attacked - then broken off the attack.
2. The existence of the base gives the party interstellar communications capability.
	1. If they ever have any problems or sight enemy ships, they will notify you no matter where you are in the region.
## Return to planet SS-20B
1. Continue as if you are going to teleport another party to the surface.  Notice the prompt "Beam landing party up?"; 
	1. press [Y].
2. The landing party display which appears shows the teams that are currently on the planet.  Since the planet survey is complete, you can recall those teams.
3. Move the highlight bar to Survey/Explore and press [-] three times so that the number of teams left reads zero.  
	1. Do the same with the negotiating teams.
	2. Now activaet the teleporters.
4. Leave the base personnel on the planet to protect the base from enemy attack.
5. If you had wanted to recall all the teams:
	1. Press [SHIFT] [A] then [ENTER]
## Conclusion
1. Go to the Warp Map
2. Navigate back to Starfort
3. Dock
4. Quit tutorial
