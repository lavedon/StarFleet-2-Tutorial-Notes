# Tutorial 8: Strategic Command

## WHEN TO USE: Prior to mission 5-A
## REFERENCE: __Krellan Command and Battle Manual__ Strategic Command - Section 10, pages 68-73

### NOTE: Sub-Admiral or higher unlocks strategic command.

## Starting Out:
1. You are in orbit around planet SS-5A and have only two escorts remaining.
2. Freeze the simulation to investigate.

## Commanding Other Krellan Ships:
1. Go to the System Map.
2. There is a red dot near the planet 
	+ This is an enemy heavy cruiser.
3. Go to the Main Display
	+ You are low on supplies.
4. Press [G] to enter Strategic Command.
	 + Press [R] for Regional Command
	 + [T] for Total
	 + [S] for Space Forces.
5. All Krellan ships are listed according to classification.
6. NOTE: There is no E2 or E3.
7. While still on page 1 of the listing, press [O] to see the orders of all these ships.
8. Notice supply ship KS4-EREIKJJI is in Free Operation at Starfort 2.
9. Move the cursor down until that ship is highlighted
	+ Then press [ENTER]
10. This displays the command window for that ship.
11. Select Rendezvous and press [ENTER].
12. You will receive a message acknowledging the order.
13. Press any key
	+ Followed by [ESC]
	+ You have now returned to the space forces listing.

## Recruiting another Battlecruiser
1. Only two battlecruisers are available.
	+ KS-0 SLAYER
	+ KS-1 CONQUEST
2. If you look at their orders you will see that they are both invading planets.
3. Move the highlight to SLAYER and order a Rendezvous

## Replace Missing Escorts
1. You now want to replace the missing escorts.
2. Press [PgDn] to move to page 2 of the status report 
3. Notice, all destroyers already have orders.
4. Go to page 3 [PgDn]
5. Notice two destroyers are in free operation
	+ KS-33 GWEZN 
	+ KS-36 ICE
6. Order them to Rendezvous 
	+ You can use the Conscript command when they arrive.
7. Press [ESC] to leave the status report.
8. Press [O] to see the total Krellan losses in this mission.
9. The two destroyers you recruited have been destroyed.
10. Press [ESC] until you reach Local/Total 
11. Press [L] and [S] to list the ships in the same star system as you.
12. Return to the Main Display.

# NOTE: The Heavy Cruiser's next actions are not scripted.  The Heavy Cruiser may:
	1. Go away
		+ Mostl likely
	2. Stay where it is 
	3. Enter orbit 

## Unfreeze tutorial
1. Unfroeeze.
2. After a few moments you will begin to receive messages about the ships you ordered to Rendezvous 
	+ Arriving in your system.
	+ Reaching orbit.
3. When the supply ship arrives
	+ Resupply your own ship
	+ Transfer some supplies to your escorts (from your newly supplied ship)
4. When the destroyer enters orbit
	+ Conscript the destroyer

## After you have conscripted new escorts 
1. After you have conscripted and resupplied
2. Target planet SS-10A at Reg Loc 24, 4 
3. Leave orbit with your new escorts.
4. If the enemy heavy cruiser is still there, 
	+ It is probably on the other side of the planet
	+ You can escape without having to commence combat
5. Enter orbit around SS-10A 
6. Order your escorts to also orbit.

## Return an escort 
1. Find a ship to send an escort to 
	+ Press [G]
	+ Press [R]
	+ Press [T]
	+ Press [S] 
2. You are now viewing the space forces list.
3. NOTE: Ship KS-26ZZCIWNSZ is not a good candidate to send a ship to.
	+ This ship is damaged, and only has 4% power.
3. We will send an escort to KS-20 IEOYY
4. Command KS-20 to Escort Ship [E]
	+ Select KS-0 as the ship to be escorted.
5. Press [ENTER] to confirm acknowledgement.

## INVASION COMMAND
1. Freeze the simulation
2. Go the Nav Map
3. Call up a Database on the star system at 20,13
4. There is a UGA planet in the system -- a good target.
5. Note the number of the planet, 48A.
	+ Press [ESC]
	+ Press [S]
	 	+ This will take you directly to Strategic Command.
6. Press [T] for Total,
7. Go to the Space Forces report.

### Order an Invasion
1. Call up the battlecruiser CONQUEST 
2. Order CONQUEST to invade planet SS-48A.
3. You can also order some troop ships to assist.
	+ Select KS-6 (JBVJ) and KS-16 (MYE) in turn and order them to invade SS-48A.

### RENDEZVOUS
1. Go to the Main Display.
2. You may have already noticed that all the ships with Rendezvous commands are still following you.
	+ They will continue to do so until ordered to stop.
	+ Or until they run out of power.
3. View the local forces and their orders.
	+ Except for your escorts (yours and those of KS-9)
	+ All the ships in orbit are commanded to Rendezvous.
	+ Order KS-0 to patrol the system on the lookout for the enemy.
	+ Order the others to return to Free Operation.
4. Quit the tutorial.
