## WHEN TO USE: Prior to mission 1-E
## REFERENCE _Krellan Command and Battle Manual_ (Weapons Control - Section 6, Pages 49-53)

### Starting Out
1. You begin in orbit around planet SS-18A with three enemy freighters.
2. Weapons are set to the default settings
	1. Automatic Targeting
	2. Set to Destroy
## Torpedoes and Phasers

1. Pause/Freeze the simulation [ALT][F]
2. Look for the letters "P T T ", in the Main Display, above the TARGET DATA.
	1. These letters are the status of your one phaser bank (P)
	2. and two torpedo tubes (T T).
3. Green means they are ready to fire.
	1. If the indicator is red, they are not ready to fire.
	2. If the indicator is black, they are not operational.
		1. Which can be caused by:
			a. Phaser or Torpedo control is seriously damaged
			b. No phaser energy left
			c. Out of Torpedoes
			
4. Just below these indicators is the Target Data Display.
	1. In the upper right corner of the display is the number of torpedoes remaining.
	2. The column labelled "R.Pos" shows the relative position of any enemy units at firing range.'
	3. "Hdng" and "Vel" tell you in which direction, and at what speed, that particular target is travelling.
	4. The "Wpn" column shows which weapon is locked on that target.
## Wpn is color coded
| Color | Setting |
| :--: | :--: |
| <span style="color:red">red</span> | destroy |
| <span style="color:yellow">yellow</span> | disable |
| <span style="color:blue">blue</span> | standby |

### targeting mode color
1. The background color of the letter tells you the targeting mode:
	1. White for Manual
	2. Black for Automatic

# Firing Your Weapons

1. Unfreeze [ALT][F]
2. Press [SHIFT][10] to fire torpedoes at two of the freighters.
	1. ==**HINT:** When firing on the move, be sure to have the [SHIFT] key down **BEFORE** you press [F10], or else your ship will stop in the midst of battle.==
3. Fire your phasers at the third freighter [SHIFT][F9].
	1. When a target is hit, you will get a report telling you the damage caused.
	2. You can not fire again util your torpedo tubes are reloaded
	or
	3. the phasers are re-energized.
	4. The indicators turn <span color:red>red</span>, briefly, while the weapons are reloading.
## Manual Firing of Torpedoes
1. Freeze [ALT][F]
2. [W] for Weapons Control
3. [T] for Torpedoes
4. Report confirms that they are at the default setting: **Auto/Destroy**

5. [S] put Torpedo #1 on Standby
	1. We will not use this torpedo just yet
6. Torpedo #2 press [M] for Mode and [M] again for Manual.
7. A TDB appears in the Orbital Display; move it to the freighter you want to target and press [ENTER].
8. Go back to the Main Menu ([ALT][M]) and note the changes in the Target Data 
	+ white box appears appears behind the second "T"
9. Unfreeze and fire your torpedoes as soon as they are ready.
10. Only one will fire and hit the target you designated.
11. You will get a message box declaring you have completed your mission -- ignore!
12. Freeze [ALT][F]
	1.  Reset **BOTH** torpedoes to Auto and Destroy.

## Disabling with your Torpedoes
1. You can set a torpedo to Disable rather than Destroy.
2. When an enemy ship is disabled, you can use the tractor beam to draw it in and board it.
	1. This only works if the enemy ship's shields are down.

## Fire Phasers in Manual Mode
1. Phasers can be fired in Manual mode just like torpedoes.
2. The setting procedure is identical.
	+ Unfreeze [ALT][F]
	+ Go to the Main Display
	+ Fire Your weapons until the freigther is destroyed or you move out of range.

# Firing Your Weapons In Normal Space
1. You can fire weapons when traveling at sublight speed
	+ Just like when you are in orbit
2. Targets must be in range:
	+ Range of torpedoes is 10 points.
	+ Range of phasers is 5 points.
3. Check the Target Data to be sure your target is in range.
4. Your Weapons Officer will tell you when your weapons are locked onto a target.

# Continuous Torpedo Fire (Barrage Mode)
1. If you have not selected a bombardment mode, you can order your Torpedo Control to fire whenever possible 
	+ i.e. when a tube is loaded and locked onto a target
2. Press [ALT][B] 
3. A flashing "Cont" will appear on the weapon indicator line.
4. This lessens your work load during combat, but you must fire phasers yourself.
5. Press [ALB][B] again to cancel **Barrage Mode**
	+ Entering hyperspace will also cancel **Barrage Mode**

# Obliterator Pods
1. Obliterator Pods are used only to destroy planets.
2. You are equipped with two Obliterator Pods
3. Each pod destroys 50% of a planet.

### Destroying planet 18A

1. From the Main Display, press [W] for Weapons
2. Press [0] to release a pod.
3. Press [Y] to confirm firing and start the countdown.
4. Launch a second planet to destroy the planet
	+ [W] Weapons
	+ [O] release pod
	+ [Y] confirm firing
	+ Wait for countdown to complete.

### Use Obliterator Pods sparingly

1. The Krellan empire needs planets to colonize and exploit.
	+ Even unattractive planets have some value.
